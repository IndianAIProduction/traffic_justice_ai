STATE-SPECIFIC RTO RULES — SOUTH INDIA
Covers: Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Puducherry, Lakshadweep

## TAMIL NADU

### Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1974
Road tax rates:
- Two-wheelers up to 100cc: Rs 1,200 (one-time)
- Two-wheelers 100-170cc: Rs 2,000 (one-time)
- Two-wheelers above 170cc: Rs 3,000 (one-time)
- Cars up to 1000cc: 6% of vehicle cost
- Cars 1000-1500cc: 8%
- Cars 1500-2000cc: 10%
- Cars above 2000cc: 12%
- Diesel surcharge: 2% additional
- Electric vehicles: 100% road tax exemption (TN EV Policy 2023)
- Hybrid vehicles: 50% road tax reduction

### Tamil Nadu-Specific Traffic Rules
(a) Tamil Nadu has partially adopted central 2019 fine rates:
    Some fines reduced by state notification:
    - No helmet (194B): Rs 500 (reduced from Rs 1,000) first offence
    - General offence (177): Rs 250 (reduced from Rs 500)
    - Overspeeding (183): Rs 1,000 (reduced from Rs 2,000) for LMV
(b) Chennai-specific rules:
    - Heavy vehicle entry: Restricted 8 AM to 10 PM in city
    - Parking: Multi-level and automated parking in key areas
    - One-way traffic system extensively used
    - Beach Road: Specific speed restrictions
(c) E-challan: Active in Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy, Salem
(d) ANPR cameras: Deployed on Chennai OMR, ECR, NH
(e) Special enforcement: During Tamil New Year, Pongal, festivals
(f) Flood protocol: Vehicle operation rules during Chennai monsoon floods

### Tamil Nadu Transport Corporation (TNSTC/SETC)
- Multiple corporations: TNSTC, SETC, MTC (Chennai city)
- Extensive network covering all districts
- Online booking: tnstc.in
- Concessional passes: Students, senior citizens, disabled persons, freedom fighters

### RTO Network
36+ RTOs across Tamil Nadu
Main RTO: Chennai (Anna Salai)
Website: tnsta.gov.in
Transport Commissioner: Chennai
Helpline: 044-25384999

### Chennai City Traffic Rules
(a) Signal-free corridor: OMR, Mount Road sections
(b) Bus rapid transit: Under development
(c) Metro rail integration: Park-and-ride facilities
(d) Coastal regulation: No parking on beach roads
(e) IT corridor speed limit: 40 km/h during peak hours

## KERALA

### Kerala Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1976
Road tax rates:
- Two-wheelers: 6% of vehicle cost (one-time, lifetime)
- Cars up to 1000cc: 6%
- Cars 1000-1500cc: 7%
- Cars 1500-2500cc: 8%
- Cars above 2500cc: 10%
- SUVs/luxury vehicles above Rs 20 lakhs: 20% (highest in India)
- Diesel vehicles: Same rates (no additional surcharge, but higher base rate for expensive vehicles)
- Electric vehicles: 50% reduction in road tax

### Kerala-Specific Traffic Rules
(a) Kerala has kept most central fine rates:
    - Drunken driving: Strictly enforced, especially during Onam and Christmas
    - Mobile phone use: Rs 5,000 — strictly enforced
(b) Monsoon/flood protocol: Road closure announcements, vehicle operation restrictions during heavy rains
(c) Hill station rules: Ghat roads — special speed limits, horn usage mandatory on blind corners
(d) Tourist vehicle permits: Strict enforcement on Munnar, Wayanad, Thekkady routes
(e) Water transport: Separate licensing for boat/ferry operations
(f) Autorickshaw fare: Strictly regulated — meter mandatory in all districts
(g) Night driving restrictions: For tourist buses on ghat roads (some sections)

### Kerala State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC)
- Extensive network ("The lifeline of Kerala")
- Online booking: keralartc.com
- Premium services: Garuda, Minnal, Swift
- Women-only buses on select routes

### Kerala RTO
14 RTOs across districts
Main RTO: Thiruvananthapuram
Website: mvd.kerala.gov.in
Helpline: 0471-2328799
Online services: Extensive — DL, RC, tax payment, fitness, all available online

### Kerala-Specific Issues
(a) High road accident rate per vehicle: Enhanced enforcement measures
(b) Drink driving: Zero tolerance during festivals
(c) Tourist bus safety: Strict fitness checks before Sabarimala season
(d) Waterlogging: Traffic diversion protocols during monsoon

## KARNATAKA

### Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957
Road tax rates:
- Two-wheelers: 10-12% of vehicle cost (one-time, lifetime)
- Cars up to Rs 5 lakhs: 13%
- Cars Rs 5-10 lakhs: 14%
- Cars Rs 10-20 lakhs: 17%
- Cars above Rs 20 lakhs: 18%
- Electric vehicles: Tax exemption under Karnataka EV Policy 2021
- Government vehicles: Exempted

### Karnataka-Specific Traffic Rules
(a) Karnataka follows central fine rates largely
(b) Bengaluru-specific rules:
    - Heavy vehicle entry: Restricted 6 AM to 10 PM (inner ring road area)
    - Auto-rickshaw fare: Minimum Rs 30, Rs 15/km thereafter (with meter)
    - Taxi aggregator cap: Maximum 2x surge during peak hours
    - Parking: Smart parking system in CBD area
    - Metro integration: Park-and-ride facilities at metro stations
    - Signal priority: For BMTC buses on select routes
(c) E-challan: Active across Bengaluru, Mysuru, Hubli-Dharwad, Mangaluru
(d) ANPR: Deployed on major Bengaluru roads and highways
(e) Eco-sensitive zones: Speed limits near Bandipur, Nagarhole national parks
(f) Night driving ban: On Bandipur stretch of NH 766 (Mysuru-Kerala highway)

### Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC)
- KSRTC and BMTC (Bengaluru city), NWKRTC (North-West), NEKRTC (North-East)
- Premium: Airavat Club Class, Ambari
- Online booking: ksrtc.in
- Concessional: Students, senior citizens, journalists

### Karnataka RTO
30+ RTOs
Main RTO: Bengaluru (Koramangala)
Website: transport.karnataka.gov.in
Helpline: 080-22942222
Online: Comprehensive online services via Kaveri/Vahan

## ANDHRA PRADESH

### AP Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1963
Road tax rates:
- Two-wheelers: 9-12% of vehicle cost (one-time)
- Cars up to Rs 10 lakhs: 12%
- Cars Rs 10-20 lakhs: 14%
- Cars above Rs 20 lakhs: 16%
- Diesel vehicles: Additional 2% surcharge
- Electric vehicles: 100% exemption (AP EV Policy)

### AP-Specific Traffic Rules
(a) AP follows central fine rates
(b) E-challan: Active in Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, Tirupati, Guntur, Kakinada
(c) New capital roads (Amaravati): Speed enforcement on new highway stretches
(d) Tirupati pilgrimage traffic: Special traffic management during festivals
(e) Coastal road rules: Speed restrictions during cyclone warnings
(f) Sand transport: Strict permits required, GPS tracking mandatory
(g) Toll-free roads: Some state highways toll-free for two-wheelers

### AP State Road Transport Corporation (APSRTC)
- Extensive network across 13 districts
- Online booking: apsrtconline.in
- Premium: Garuda Plus, Indra, Vennela (night services)
- Tirupati special services

### AP RTO
26+ RTOs
Main RTO: Vijayawada
Website: aptransport.org
Helpline: 0866-2490433

## TELANGANA

### Telangana Motor Vehicles Taxation Act
Road tax rates (similar to AP with some variations):
- Two-wheelers: 9-12%
- Cars up to Rs 10 lakhs: 12%
- Cars Rs 10-20 lakhs: 14%
- Cars above Rs 20 lakhs: 16%
- Electric vehicles: 100% road tax exemption (Telangana EV Policy 2020-2030)

### Telangana-Specific Traffic Rules
(a) Telangana follows central fine rates
(b) Hyderabad-specific rules:
    - Traffic Integration: Hyderabad Traffic Police app (HTRIP)
    - E-challan: Fully electronic, AI-based violation detection
    - Speed cameras: Extensive network on ORR (Outer Ring Road) and NH
    - Heavy vehicle entry: Restricted in inner city 7 AM to 10 PM
    - Auto fare: Minimum Rs 25, Rs 12/km
    - IT corridor (HITEC City): Special traffic arrangements during peak hours
(c) ANPR cameras: Deployed across Hyderabad, Cyberabad area
(d) Real-time traffic monitoring: Command and control centre
(e) Drone surveillance: For major events and congestion monitoring

### Telangana State Road Transport Corporation (TSRTC)
- Online booking: tsrtconline.in
- Premium: Garuda Plus, Super Luxury
- Concessional passes available

### Telangana RTO
31+ RTOs
Main RTO: Hyderabad (Khairatabad)
Website: transport.telangana.gov.in
Helpline: 040-23262669
Cyberabad Traffic Police: 040-27853530

## PUDUCHERRY (Union Territory)

### Puducherry Motor Vehicles Tax
Road tax rates (generally lower than neighbouring states):
- Two-wheelers: 4-6%
- Cars: 5-8%
- Electric vehicles: Reduced rates

### Puducherry-Specific Rules
(a) Follows central fine rates
(b) Tourist destination: Special traffic management during French Quarter events
(c) Beach road: Speed restrictions, no heavy vehicles
(d) Auroville area: Special vehicle restrictions
(e) Smaller RTO — simpler procedures

### Puducherry RTO
Website: transport.py.gov.in
Single main RTO office

## LAKSHADWEEP (Union Territory)

### Lakshadweep Transport Rules
(a) Very limited motorised transport
(b) Electric vehicles encouraged
(c) Speed limits very low (island roads)
(d) Registration through UT administration
(e) Minimal traffic enforcement (low vehicle density)
(f) Boats/ferries: Separate licensing under maritime rules
